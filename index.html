How to read these cards
⚠ Scores are measured against simulated historical data, not real draw results. There will always be a "best" line because it is a relative ranking — it does not mean those numbers are more likely to win.
Big number = Close Score
How close this line got to real draw results over the last 4 draws. Each full number match = 2 pts. Each number ±1 away = 0.3 pts. Higher is better.
★ Best / ✓ Good / Fair badges
Ranks the 5 lines by Close Score. The line with the highest Close Score gets ★ Best. Badges update each week as new draw results come in.
Confidence % & Signal
Secondary metrics. Confidence = how this line ranked during generation (frequency + sum + spread). Signal = the raw internal score before normalisation. Neither is a win probability.
Selection Methodology
Frequency Weighting
Numbers appearing more often in the last 52 draws receive proportionally higher selection weight.
Recency Bias
Last 12 draws are weighted 2× vs older draws, capturing recent pattern shifts without overfitting.
Gap Analysis
Numbers overdue relative to their average frequency gap receive a modest upward adjustment.
Diversity Constraint
No two adjacent lines may share more than 2 numbers, ensuring spread across the number pool.
Sum-Range Filter
Generated lines must fall within the historical median sum range ±15% to avoid extreme combinations.
Draw Lock
EuroMillions picks lock per draw (Tue/Fri) and expire at 20:00 on draw day. UK Lotto picks lock per calendar week. Numbers are stored in localStorage and never regenerated mid-cycle.
